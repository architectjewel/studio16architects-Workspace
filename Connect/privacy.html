Privacy Policy

S16A Connect — Studio 16 Architects · Last updated: 9 July 2026
S16A Connect is a private, internal business application for authorised staff of Studio 16 Architects (Sync Land Limited). It is not intended for use by the general public, and accounts are issued by the company. The app is not directed to children under 13.

Information we collect

The app collects only the information needed to record company expenditure and purchasing:

  • Account information — your name, staff/employee identifier, email address and role, as issued by Studio 16 Architects.
  • Work records you enter — cash advances, expenses, requisitions, purchase orders, deliveries, challans and payment entries, including amounts, dates, notes, vendors and projects.
  • Authentication data — a secure session token used to keep you signed in.

The app does not collect location data, contacts, photos, device identifiers for advertising, or any personal data unrelated to your work records.

How we use it

  • To authenticate you and control which screens and actions your role permits.
  • To record, display and reconcile company financial and purchasing records.
  • To maintain an audit trail showing who recorded or verified each entry.

We do not use your information for advertising or profiling, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

Where data is stored

Data is stored in a private database hosted by Supabase and served over an encrypted HTTPS connection. Access is restricted to authenticated staff accounts and to the administrators of Studio 16 Architects. Records are retained for as long as required for the company's accounting and legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties, except service providers that host the application (Supabase for the database, Vercel for hosting), which process data solely on our instructions, or where disclosure is required by law.

Your choices

Because this is an internal company system, accounts and records are managed by Studio 16 Architects. If you would like to access, correct or delete information about you, or ask a question about this policy, contact us and we will respond.

Security

Access requires a password-protected account. All traffic is encrypted in transit. Sensitive actions (such as verifying a bill or payment) are limited to administrators, and entries record the identity of the person who created them.

Deleting your account

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time. See Delete your account for the steps and for what is deleted or retained.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the date shown above.
