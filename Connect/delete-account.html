S16A Connect — Studio 16 Architects and Sync Land
S16A Connect is an internal application. Accounts are created and managed by Studio 16 Architects and Sync Land Limited for its own staff. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time using the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to the address below from the email address linked to your S16A Connect account.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my account
  3. Include your full name and staff identifier so we can locate the correct account.

Email a deletion request

We confirm receipt within 7 days and complete verified requests within 30 days.

What is deleted

Deleted Your user account, sign-in credentials, name, email address, role, and your ability to access the app.
Retained Company financial records you created while employed — cash advances, expenses, requisitions, purchase orders, deliveries and payments. These are business accounting records of Studio 16 Architects and Sync Land, and are retained as required by Bangladeshi tax and company law, normally for six years. After your account is deleted these records are no longer linked to your sign-in credentials.
